About:

Atelier Chapuis was created by Mark Chapuis in 2024 to serve as an incubator for his projects. This website is the gateway to the details of each project.

Having a dedicated space to develop and test new ideas before they are ready for market is essential for any company aiming to innovate. Such a space provides the focused environment needed to transform raw ideas into fully developed solutions. It allows for uninterrupted experimentation, where concepts can be tested, refined, and iterated upon, ensuring the final product is robust, well-tested, and most importantly - fits customers' needs.

This dedicated space fosters creativity and experimentation, offering the flexibility to explore different approaches and pivot freely and without consequence.

The ultimate goal of Atelier Chapuis is to develop new products to a state of maturity that enables them to be successsfully spun-off into their own independent companies.
